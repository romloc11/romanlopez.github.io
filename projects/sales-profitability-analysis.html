Sales & Profit Analysis

Business Context

The company needed better visibility into its commercial performance across regions and product categories.

Problem

There was no clear visualization to identify which regions generated the highest sales and profitability.

Solution

A dashboard was developed in Power BI to analyze:

  • Total sales performance
  • Profitability by product
  • Geographical performance
  • Sales trends over time

Impact

The dashboard enabled the business to focus commercial strategies on the most profitable regions and identify opportunities for improvement.
