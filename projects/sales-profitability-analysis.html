Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

Business Context

Understanding sales performance and profitability is essential for organizations to make strategic decisions about pricing, product strategy, and regional market focus.

The objective of this analysis was to create a centralized analytical dashboard that allows business stakeholders to evaluate commercial performance through key metrics and visual insights.

Problem

Before the dashboard was developed, decision makers lacked a clear and consolidated view of sales and profitability across regions, products, and time periods.

Data existed in multiple tables but required manual analysis to answer questions such as:

  • Which regions generate the highest revenue?
  • Which products contribute the most to overall profitability?
  • How do sales evolve over time?
  • Where are the most profitable market opportunities?

Data Preparation

Data was imported and transformed using Power BI Power Query. Several data preparation steps were implemented to ensure reliable analysis:

  • Cleaning and standardizing product and regional information
  • Handling missing values and inconsistent records
  • Creating calculated measures for financial metrics
  • Structuring the dataset for analytical modeling

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard focuses on several business KPIs used to evaluate commercial performance:

  • Total Sales Revenue
  • Total Profit
  • Profit Margin
  • Sales Growth Over Time
  • Regional Sales Distribution
  • Product Category Performance

Dashboard Design

The dashboard was designed using Power BI to provide an intuitive interface for exploring sales and profitability data. Visual components were structured to support both high-level monitoring and detailed exploration.

  • Sales trends over time
  • Regional performance analysis
  • Product-level profitability insights
  • Interactive filtering for deeper analysis

Impact

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify high-performing products and regions while detecting opportunities for optimization.

  • Improved visibility of sales and profitability KPIs
  • Faster identification of top-performing regions
  • Better understanding of product profitability
  • Data-driven support for commercial strategy
